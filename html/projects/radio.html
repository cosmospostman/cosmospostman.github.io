Interviews

Radio interviews tend to be aimed at a general audience, who may not have prior knowledge of the subject. The goal then, is to hold and steer a conversation on behalf of the audience, prompting the subject to say interesting things about themselves.

Online & Software Engineering

KFJC website – I completely redesigned it for the first time in 15 years. The layout better suits modern desktop and mobile form factors; content is more accessible; and listen time on the first-party popup player (which I also redesigned) increased 2.5x with the new layout.

KFJC Android App – I built a native Android app to access KFJC’s live stream, playlists and two-week archive of recent shows. It has an average rating of 4.8 stars and ~1000 installs on active devices.
