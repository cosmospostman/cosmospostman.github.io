Bespoke Content for Schools

Let me build a presentation or workshop based on your students' needs and interests. I can bring relevance and perspective to your classroom content — tell me what you'd like covered or just point to sections of the Australian Curriculum you're visiting this semester.

While I can work with any subject matter, my personal strengths are in maths, physics (light, sound, forces & energy), computer science & software engineering, the nature of science and how we know & discover things.

For Researchers & Institutions

Your work or research can, and should, meaningfully touch communities beyond paywalled journal publications and fleeting broadcast media splashes. There's lots to love about community engagement and participation:

  • Building sentimental & financial support for your work
  • Fostering trust in science broadly in our communities
  • Opening up to the needs and views of others to inform your work
  • Making it easy to persuade or influence, or inform policy, should your research suggest so

I can inspire you to broaden your research impact and build your capacity to do so. Or, if your time is limited, I can develop and deliver novel experiential content based on your work and then tell your story during my own outreach. Let's discuss the possibilities.
